Account & Data Deletion

Hubnex AI gives users full control over their data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated information directly from inside the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Hubnex AI
  2. Tap the menu (☰) icon on the top left
  3. Open Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

The following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account information (email, name, UID)
  • Authentication data
  • Chat history and AI messages
  • Usage statistics and token data
  • Any stored preferences

Deletion is immediate and cannot be reversed.
