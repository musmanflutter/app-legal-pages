Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

hubnex AI (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: email, name, unique user ID, and authentication provider (email/password or Google).
  • Chat Data: all messages you send and AI responses generated in the app.
  • Usage Data: account creation date, updates, and tokens/usage limits (for freemium features in future).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI chat functionality and maintain your chat history.
  • Route your prompts to the appropriate AI models (OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek).
  • Manage account features, usage limits, and future subscription services.
  • Improve the app experience and troubleshoot issues.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Chat prompts are sent to third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek) to generate responses. Other than AI providers, your data remains private and secure.

4. Data Retention

Your chat history and account data are stored permanently unless you delete your account. Usage limits and tokens will be stored for freemium management.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

hubnex AI is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
